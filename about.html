Common questions

EVERYTHING WE GET ASKED · ANSWERED HONESTLY
?Is this gambling or a betting platform?
No. Pure entertainment. AI-driven race simulation, no betting, no token wagers. Think F1-style storytelling for memecoins. Race outcomes are part of the show, not a wager.
?Do we have to give you tokens or pay anything?
No. Zero cost to join. We don't ask for your token allocation, we don't charge fees, we don't require purchases of $MEMEGP. We build the platform; you show up for the show.
?What's in it for you, then?
Our platform grows when communities engage. The bigger the grid, the bigger the league, the more people watching. $MEMEGP ecosystem benefits from network effects, not from extracting value out of teams.
?What happens if our team loses?
Losing teams generate as much content as winning teams. Crashes, last-place memes, comeback storylines — every team gets coverage. The narrative of climbing back up is often louder than dominating from the front.
?Do you own our IP / branding?
No. The team is named after your coin and references your brand, but we don't claim your IP. Your community's character, art direction, and lore stay yours.
?Can we leave / withdraw at any time?
Yes. No contracts, no lock-ins. If your community decides this isn't for you, we remove your team from the grid. We'd rather have engaged participants than reluctant ones.
?Who's behind MEME GP?
A small team operating under the MEME GP banner. Public-facing through @MemeGrandPrix. We're memecoin-native, F1-obsessed, and building this because the racing-meets-memecoin format hasn't been done well yet.
?How do we get our community on the grid?
DM @MemeGrandPrix. Tell us about your community, your character, your X handle. We'll vet for fit (brand alignment, engagement, no rug history) and reach back. 12 seats remain for Season 2.
