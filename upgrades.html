Rules of the Road

WHAT COUNTS · WHAT DOESN'T
✓
Organic engagement only. Real accounts, real likes, real RTs.
✓
Authenticity reviewed. Spam patterns, engagement pods, and obvious bot rings get filtered before counting.
✓
Lowest stat already 9.5? The system picks the next-lowest stat instead.
✓
Threshold-based, not score-based. 50 likes = +1. 500 likes = +1. The cap is the cap.
✕
No bot likes, no paid pods, no spam. If it looks engineered, it gets ignored.
✕
No carryover. Miss the threshold by Friday and the stat doesn't move that cycle.
✕
No off-platform engagement. Only X likes/RTs on the official upgrade tweet count.
✕
No retroactive upgrades. The cycle closes Friday 23:59 UTC. Stats commit Saturday.

06

Paddock Questions

EVERYTHING WORTH ASKING
WHY TWO STATS · NOT ALL FIVE?
To keep it focused. One tweet, two clear targets, two clear thresholds. If every stat were on the table every week, the engagement bar gets diluted and the cycles stop meaning anything. Two lowest stats = the ones that need it most = clearest path to a faster car.
WHAT IF MY TEAM'S TWO LOWEST STATS ARE TIED?
We break the tie alphabetically by stat name (Aero before Chassis before Drag, etc.). It's a coin flip dressed up — but it keeps things deterministic and visible.
CAN I UPGRADE A TEAM I DON'T HOLD?
Yes. The system doesn't check wallets. Anyone on X can like or RT the official upgrade tweet. The only gate is authenticity — real engagement from real accounts. The loudest garage wins, not the deepest one.
WHAT IF A TWEET OVER-PERFORMS?
No bonus upgrades. Max +1 per stat per week. 500 likes earns the same upgrade as 50. Once the threshold's crossed, the stat moves. Anything above is bragging rights — and that's the point. We want communities pushing every cycle, not stockpiling one viral tweet.
HOW DO YOU HANDLE BOT ENGAGEMENT?
We manually review for authenticity before counting. Obvious spam clusters, brand-new accounts with no posting history, coordinated reply rings, paid engagement pods — all filtered. Public guideline: engagement reviewed for authenticity. The spirit: real fans, real cars, real races.
DOES THIS ACTUALLY AFFECT RACE OUTCOMES?
Yes — directly. Stats feed into the simulation engine. A team that consistently hits its upgrades will pull ahead in lap times, tyre management, and overtakes. A team that ignores its cycles falls behind. The championship will be won partly on track, partly on timeline.
WHEN DOES THE FIRST LIVE CYCLE RUN?
We're soft-launching the system now so everyone gets familiar with it. The first live upgrade cycle — where stats actually move — runs the Monday after Race 0. Exact date locks when the Race 0 schedule drops.
WHAT ABOUT TEAMS THAT JOIN MID-SEASON?
Same rules apply from the moment they're locked in. They get their first upgrade cycle the Monday after they join. Late starters can absolutely catch up — five upgrade cycles per stat is a lot of headroom.
