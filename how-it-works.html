The 5 Stats

EVERY STAT IS DERIVED FROM YOUR COIN'S ON-CHAIN DATA
ENGINE
RAW POWER
Source log10(MCAP) normalized 1-10
Higher market cap = bigger engine. Top end speed on the straights.
AERO
DOWNFORCE
Source 24h Volume / MCAP
Trading velocity = aero efficiency. Cornering speed in the technical sectors.
CHASSIS
STIFFNESS
Source log10(Liquidity USD)
Liquidity depth = chassis rigidity. Corner stability. Doesn't get punted off track.
DRAG
STABILITY
Source 100 - abs(24h price change %)
Less volatility = lower drag. Steady straight-line speed. Doesn't blow up under load.
PIT
CREW + TYRES
Source Community engagement score
Holders + engagement + community size. Faster pit stops + slower tyre wear. Two strategic levers, one stat.

How Communities Drive the Race

EVERY STAT IS A COMMUNITY ACTION · YOUR CREW BUILDS THE CAR

Your car isn't built by us. It's built by everything your community does between Friday and Friday — trading, holding, growing, posting, defending. Every action moves the needle on a different stat.

ENGINE
GROW THE COMMUNITY
Raising your market cap raises your engine. Onboard more wallets, drive new buyers, expand the holder base — top-end speed comes from the size of the army.
AERO
TRADE THE COIN
High volume relative to mcap = aero efficiency. Active markets, real trading, communities moving in and out — every trade tunes the downforce.
CHASSIS
HOLD THE LINE
Liquidity depth = chassis stiffness. Diamond hands, deep LP, no panic selling — the more stable the holder base, the harder your car is to knock off-line.
DRAG
DEFEND THE PRICE
Less volatility = less drag. Calm, confident, organised communities hold price action steady — the car doesn't shake at top speed.
PIT
SHOW UP LOUDLY
Posts, memes, replies, race-day energy on @MemeGrandPrix. Loud communities = faster pit stops. Loyal holders = slower tyre wear. Two strategic levers, both controlled by your crew.
▸ THE BIG INSIGHT
Big-cap coins start with raw ENGINE. But AERO, CHASSIS, DRAG, and PIT are won by communities, not market caps. A scrappy coin with a rabid community can outclass the giants in 4 of 5 stats — and that's where championships are decided.
